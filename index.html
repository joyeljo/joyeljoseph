About Me
Designing Is My Passion

I'm Joyel Joseph. I'm a UI/UX designer. I have a bachelor's degree in Computer & Information Technology with a concentration in web designing from Chestnut Hill College, Philadelphia. I first got into this because I loved graphic designs, I loved creating things and I love designing things. Around 2015, is when I really started to understand the UX design process and the importance of user-centered design. My career started as a UX designer started in the summer of 2015 when I joined my college's web development team. Now, I have over 5 years of professional experience in user interface websites & applications, interactive prototyping, visual design & UX research. I have experience working with different domains including banking, education, commercial, industrial, and marketing. I have experience in working in an Agile/Scrum environment. I have good hands-on experience with Adobe creative suite, Invision, Axure RP, UX Pin, Figma, and other similar designing platforms. I also have development skills and I'm able to do HTML, CSS & JavaScript. I have experience in identifying defects, retesting the fixes, and reporting the results. I also have experience in delivering design reviews and product presentations. I'm looking forward to exploring more projects in UI/UX designs.
